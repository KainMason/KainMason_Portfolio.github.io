Professional Self-Assessment

Completing the Computer Science program at Southern New Hampshire University and developing this ePortfolio has given me the opportunity to clearly articulate my growth as a computing professional. This portfolio represents more than a collection of assignments—it reflects my progression from learning foundational concepts to applying software engineering, algorithmic thinking, database design, and security principles in realistic, industry-aligned projects.

Throughout the program, I strengthened my skills in full-stack development, problem solving, and data management. Revisiting earlier projects during CS-499 helped me shift from a “make it work” mindset to a professional focus on maintainability, scalability, documentation, and secure design.

Across the artifacts in this portfolio, I demonstrate professional communication through structured narratives and documentation, applied algorithms and data structures in performance-aware contexts, and database practices that prioritize validation and reliability. I also apply a security mindset by anticipating failures and tightening how systems handle errors and data exposure.
